Rotisserie Chicken

Description

A chicken dish that is cooked on a rotisserie by using direct heat in which the chicken is placed next to the heat source. The rotisserie spit allows for an even contact the heating elements.

Ingredients

  • 1 whole chickne
  • 1 pinch salt
  • 1/4 cup melted butter
  • 1 tablespoon Old Bay Seasoning

Utilities

  • Kalorik Maxx Air Fryer & Oven

Steps

  1. Clean out the cavity of your whole chicken, removing any giblets from inside.
  2. Season chicken generously with the remaining spices.
  3. Truss chicken with butcher's twine or other cotton string (never use nylon) to ensure the chicken stays in the proper position while fixed on the spit.
  4. Set chicken into rotisserie spit.
  5. Select Chicken on Kalorik Maxx.
  6. Once done, let rest for 5 mins before serving.
